Refund and Return Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

Customer Satisfaction Guarantee: We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help.

1. Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, the following conditions must be met:

  • The item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • The item must be in the original packaging with all tags attached
  • The return request must be initiated within 7 days of receiving the product
  • Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation) must be provided
  • Items must not be custom-made or personalized

2. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned, including:

  • Gift cards and vouchers
  • Custom-made or personalized jewelry
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Products that have been resized or altered
  • Items without original packaging or certificates
  • Products damaged due to misuse or negligence

3. Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Email us at support@mygold916.com or call +91-XXX-XXX-XXXX within 7 days of delivery
  2. Provide Details: Include your order number, reason for return, and photos of the item (if damaged or defective)
  3. Receive Authorization: We will send you a Return Authorization Number (RAN) and return instructions
  4. Ship the Item: Carefully package the item with all original materials and ship it to the address provided
  5. Track Your Return: Use a trackable shipping service and keep your tracking number

4. Refund Policy

4.1 Inspection and Approval

Once we receive your returned item, our team will inspect it within 3-5 business days. We will notify you via email about the approval or rejection of your refund.

4.2 Refund Processing

If your return is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed to your original payment method
  • Processing time: 5-10 business days from approval
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the refund is processed
  • Bank processing may take an additional 3-7 business days

4.3 Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be granted in the following cases:

  • Items with obvious signs of use or wear
  • Items returned more than 7 days after delivery
  • Items not in original packaging
  • Items with missing accessories or certificates

5. Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for:

  • Size/Design: Different size or design of the same product (subject to availability)
  • Defective Items: Replacement with identical item or store credit
  • Wrong Item: Correct item will be shipped at no additional cost

To request an exchange, follow the same process as returns. Exchange items will be shipped once we receive and inspect your returned item.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us immediately (within 48 hours of delivery)
  • Provide photos of the damage or defect
  • We will arrange for a replacement or full refund
  • Return shipping costs will be covered by us
Important: Do not return damaged or defective items without contacting us first. We may arrange for inspection or pickup.

7. Shipping Costs

  • Customer Returns: You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or we sent the wrong item
  • Defective/Wrong Items: We cover all shipping costs for replacements
  • Exchanges: Original shipping is non-refundable; new shipping charges may apply for exchanges

8. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your order:

  • Before Shipping: Full refund within 24 hours
  • After Shipping: Follow return policy once item is received
  • Custom Orders: Cannot be cancelled once production begins

9. Store Credit

In certain cases, we may offer store credit instead of a refund:

  • Store credit has no expiration date
  • Can be used for any future purchases
  • Cannot be exchanged for cash
  • Transferable as gift certificates

10. Gold Price Fluctuation

Please note that refund amounts are calculated based on:

  • The original purchase price (not current market price)
  • Gold price fluctuations do not affect refund amounts
  • Making charges are non-refundable for custom items

11. Quality Assurance

All our products undergo strict quality checks. We guarantee:

  • 916 hallmark certification for gold items
  • Authenticity certificates
  • Proper weight and purity
  • 30-day quality guarantee from date of purchase

12. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund yet:

  1. Check your bank account again
  2. Contact your credit card company (processing may take time)
  3. Contact your bank (processing delays are common)
  4. If you've done all of this and still haven't received your refund, contact us at support@mygold916.com

13. International Returns

For international orders:

  • Return shipping is the customer's responsibility
  • Customs fees are non-refundable
  • Items must clear customs inspection
  • Extended processing time may apply
